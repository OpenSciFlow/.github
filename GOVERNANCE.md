# Governance

OpenSciFlow starts with lightweight governance.

## Roles

- Curator: reviews related project entries.
- Manifest reviewer: reviews `opensciflow.yaml` examples.
- Workflow reviewer: reviews workflow templates.
- Reproducibility reviewer: reviews metadata and run-record requirements.
- Maintainer: merges PRs and coordinates releases.

## Decision process

For v0.1, decisions are made by rough consensus in GitHub issues/discussions. Maintainers should document final decisions in the relevant repo.

## Authorship policy

White paper authorship requires substantial intellectual contribution to framing, standard design, architecture, or writing. Smaller contributions should be acknowledged.
