# Code of Conduct

OpenSciFlow follows the Contributor Covenant spirit: be respectful, constructive, and evidence-focused.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss technical and scientific disagreements with concrete evidence.
- Credit upstream tools, papers, data, and contributors.
- Be explicit about limitations and uncertainty.
- Help newcomers improve contributions without gatekeeping.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Misrepresenting related projects as official partners.
- Removing license or citation information.
- Making unsupported clinical, diagnostic, or drug-development claims.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove issues/PRs that violate this code. Serious concerns can be reported through the security/contact channel listed in `SECURITY.md`.
